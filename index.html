Is my data secure with AI World?

Absolutely. We use AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. We are SOC2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Your data is used ONLY to run your workflows.

Do I need to know how to code?

Not at all. AI World is designed for business operators. You can build complex automation rules using natural language and our visual drag-and-drop interface.

How many integrations do you support?

We support over 100+ native integrations including Slack, Salesforce, Notion, HubSpot, GitHub, and Jira. We also have a robust API for custom connections.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, there are no long-term contracts. You can cancel, upgrade, or downgrade your plan at any time through your dashboard.

Starter users get access to our community forums. Pro users get priority email support, and Enterprise customers receive a dedicated Slack channel and account manager.
